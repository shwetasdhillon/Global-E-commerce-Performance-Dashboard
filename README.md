# Global-E-commerce-Performance-Dashboard
A 3‑year analytics project exploring sales, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance across 20 countries.
This project analyzes 2,000 synthetic e‑commerce transactions from January 2023 to December 2025, covering multiple product categories, customer segments, and global regions. The dataset was designed for business intelligence practice, and this dashboard transforms it into a clear, interactive decision‑making tool.

The goal was to demonstrate end‑to‑end BI capability: data modeling, DAX calculations, time‑intelligence, visualization design, and storytelling.

🎯 Key Business Questions Answered

How do sales and profit trends evolve over time?

Which regions and countries contribute the most revenue?

Which product categories and customer segments drive profitability?

How do discounts impact margins?

Are there seasonal patterns in ordering behavior?

What are the preferred payment methods across regions?

How do shipping costs affect profit margins?
